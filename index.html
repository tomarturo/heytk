Solutions Marketing

VistaPrint • 2023

Helping small business owners accomplish their marketing goals.

Small business owners are overwhelmed with options and crunched for time.

They don't know where to go to find the products they need.

It is difficult to obtain multiple products that work well together.

hypothesis

Collections of products in service of accomplishing common small business goals will help customers find what they need, discover new products, and increase awareness of Vista's cross-category offerings.

role & process

Embedded in a cross-functional squad of twenty people, I represented UX alongside two designers.

We facilitated a week long workshop, then generated and tested three initial concepts in a two week discovery sprint.

Over the next four months we iterated, diverged, and refined based on existing research, user testing, and various engineering and content constraints.

We presented bi-weekly to the larger team, leveraging our findings to influence experiment scope and project strategy.

Field Reports

The Dyrt • 2022

Gathering cell coverage and campground data with a touch of delight.

Campers want to know if they'll have cell service before they reserve a campsite.

Staying connected lowers the barrier of entry to the outdoors.

Competitors are collecting and displaying this data on their listings.

role & process

I owned this feature from start to finish, working closely with the Head of Product and a Lead Engineer.

I was given two requirements: measure network connection speed and gather amenities data points.

As a part of this work, I also audited the app navigation patterns.

We standardized control placement, iconography, and UX copy in S'mores, the design system.

This feature was an opportunity to unite interaction design, a technical requirement, and The Dyrt's evolving brand identity.

I collaborated with our in-house illustrator to combine our visual style and easy-going brand voice into a one-of-a-kind loading indicator.

After the connection test, users have the option of answering amenities questions about the campground.

Leveraging our custom iconography and some motion, this interaction breathes life and delight into an otherwise tedious task.

The Field Report feature extends the customer experience of the app beyond planning and into the camping moment.
